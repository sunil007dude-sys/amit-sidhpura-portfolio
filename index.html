A Career Built on
Gems & Leadership

Amit N. Sidhpura is a Senior Sales & Operations Leader with 19 years of uninterrupted growth within the International Gemological Institute — one of the world's most respected names in gem certification and education.

Starting as a Loose Diamond Grader and rising through every rung — Team Leader, Assistant Manager, Manager of Training & Development, and now Senior Manager for Pan-India and the Middle East — his journey is a rare testament to industry depth and sustained leadership excellence.

He combines deep gemological expertise (Graduate Gemologist – IGI; Diploma in Diamond Grading – Gem-A London) with sharpened commercial acumen (Senior Management Program – IIM Calcutta) to deliver measurable outcomes across multi-region P&L, institutional partnerships, and team capability development.
